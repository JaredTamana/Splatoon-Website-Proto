Nogami Interviewed on Future of Splatoon: "We’re not yet at a phase where we can make a promise"


July 17, 2019


In an interview between Japanese magazine Famitsu, Splatoon series producer Hisashi Nogami says the next entry in the series has not yet entered development.


“We’re not yet at a phase where we can make a promise, but we’d like to return the favour in some sort of way to many people who have supported us,” Nogami said. “If you continue to support us, it might turn into reality.”
