The Community

Users can post handwritten notes and art using the game's built-in bulletin system.

Online

Splatoon is inherently a social experience. From everywhere between unofficial online forums, to in-game social media posts, to integration with Facebook and Twitter, and even integrations with other services like Battlefy that help the competitive community thrive, this may possibly be one of Nintendo's most socially-focused games.

The Competitive Scene

Splatoon is one of the three main games Nintendo makes a consistent effort to promote competitive play in, with the others being Super Smash Brothers Ultimate and ARMS. Nintendo holds official tournaments, both invite-only and global, so that fans of the game can compete and show their skills.
