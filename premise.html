Splatoon's Premise

Art of an Inkling, by u/avesDSN.

In a world...

...where humans have long perished, sealife has taken over, evolving and growing its' own culture in place. Jellyfish, urchins, whales, anemones... and of course, Inklings and Octolings, all highly intelligent sea creatures that have adapted to their post-dystopian environment.

In modern day, Inkling fashion and pop culture is at its peak. The main pastime of young Inklings, Turf Wars, evolved as a sport inspired by a war long ago between the races of Inkling and Octoling.

You, the player, rise up in rank in Turf Wars, gaining enough money along the way to upgrade your gear and weapons.

What's a Turf War?

Despite the serious name, Turs Wars are all in good fun! They are sporting events held between cephalopods with different objectives. Each player's weapon of choice spreads their ink, which is coloured to match their team. This ink damages the other team and slows them down -- and if sprayed with it, sends them back to their beginning spawn, pushing them back further from their objective.
