Splatoon's Lore

Spoilers Ahead!

Judd the Cat's cryogenic pod, as seen in Sunken Scroll 1-27.

10,000 years ago...

...humans knew the sea levels were rising. The longest-living known being, Judd the Cat, was sealed in a crygenic chamber by his owner. Not long after this, humans became extinct. For thousands of years, surface life was scarce. Then gave rise to the Inklings.

The evolution of Inklings, adapting to the surface.

At some point, sea levels rose again. This caused feuding between the Inklings and their relatives, the Octarians. The fight over the land became known as The Great Turf War. The Octarians had the upper hand, but lost due to carelessness. They were banished to Valleys and Canyons while Inklings lived in the higher society.

The events of the first game

Callie and Marie are two pop superstars who grew up in Calimari Country (no, I am not making this up). They are the artists of a song known to have magical powers, bringing the best out of anyone who hears it. The duo is heralded in Inkling pop culture.

The Squid Sisters Callie (left) and Marie (right) in studio.

The two begin to host Inkopolis News, announcing stages and events happening in the game world. However, they live a double life, helping out their grandfather, Cuttlefish when the Inkling world's power supply, a living being known as the Great Zapfish, is stolen by the Octarians. The player character, known as Agent 3, assists them.

The difficult choice put to players in Splatoon's Final Splatfest.

A monthy occurence in Inkling culture is the Splatfest. Players choose a side to battle for such as "cats or dogs". These Splatfests were initially held by Callie and Marie, until the two retired from Inkopolis News. As such, they hosted a Final Splatfest, where players were to choose between either of the girls. Marie's team won, causing Callie to go on a trip and subsequently missing.

The events of Splatoon 2

With the Great Zapfish stolen again, a new Agent is recruited. At the end of this rainbow, though, it is found that Callie is brainwashed by an Octarian. With the help of Marie, Agent 4 manages to save her.

The weapon wielded by the rogue AI, the NILS Statue.

Meanwhile, however, something sinister is happening underground. Cuttlefish stumbles upon an Octoling who has heard Callie and Marie's song, coming to her senses. Trapped inside a test vault, the Octoling is forced to go through trials to earn her freedom. Turning out to be a trap by an AI attempting to cause a genocide, the Octoling saves Inkling society with the help of Cuttlefish and pop duo Off The Hook.

Just recently, Off The Hook hosted their Final Splatfest, Order vs Chaos. It looks like it may have dire implications on the next part of the plot.
